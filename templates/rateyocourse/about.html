About Our Team


This website was created for you to be able to rate your professors and courses without the worry of procrations. We aim to help you to be able to gain an objective view of the professor and courses you are about to take. Or if you are a new studnet then you can gain an objective view of your chosen university. We verify all users before they are able to comment or rate courses and universities. However, it is not meant to offend people, so please use the cite responsively.

Our team came to existence during our university years. We realized that student experience with given courses and universities is vital, however, it is hard to gain excess to this information without having pepole who you know and already took the courses. This website lets you skip this stage and allows you to gain the information you need, without knowing people.


How to use the cite?


You can view courses and universities, as well as, read comments related to the given university or course. However, to be able to write reviews and rate, you have to sign in. If you are not yet a member, you can register. If you are already a member, you would not be reading this page (but you can sign in under the page).
